Chicago Traffic Analysis


The purpose of this project was to study traffic in the City of Chicago, including congestion, crash, and traffic camera data, all of which has been made publicly available by the City of Chicago.

We gathered data from data.cityofchicago.org, via the Socrata Open Data API (SODA) or direct .csv download, imported and reviewed data using Python and the Pandas library, and created visualizations with Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Google Maps.
