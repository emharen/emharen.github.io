Red Light/Speed Camera Locations




Here the Red Light and Speed Cameras are overlayed onto the traffic crash map. Due to the lack of available crash data prior to camera installation, it was not possible to prove causation or effect of camera installation on crash levels. However, the graphs below do show a correlation between the incidents of a crash and the number of cameras.


Strong correlation between number of red light cameras and crashes
r = 0.80
p = 2.35e-07


Strong correlation between number of speed cameras and crashes
r = 0.71
p = 1.43e-05
